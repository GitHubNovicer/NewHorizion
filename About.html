About Me

Parker Alexander

I thought I'd make it personal and talk about myself a little bit. I am solid in my belief about Jesus and the Bible being true. I love talking to God day in and day out about my troubles and mistakes, successes and challenges. God inhabits our praise. Praise be to Jesus the Most High King.

In my early years of faith, I definitely struggled more with sinning, but sin diminishes as you grow. I still battle with anxiety and fear when I do fall, but I turn to Jesus in faith for the forgiveness of my sin. Repent! I turn away from sin unto God; sometimes my repentance is short lived other times its stands the test of time. He comforts me in my brokeness and leads me by still waters.
